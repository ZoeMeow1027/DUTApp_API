MainViewModel & IsProcessingData
- NewsGlobal: Is processing or result in news global
- NewsGlobalPage: Current news global page
- NewsSubject: Is processing or result in news subject
- NewsSubjectPage: Current news subject page
- SubjectSchedule: Is processing or result in subject schedule
- SubjectFee: Is processing or result in subject fee
- AccInfo: Is processing or result in account information
- AccLoginStartup: Is auto logging in at startup
- LoggingIn: Check if login is in progress
- SettingsPanelIndex: Current panel in settings pane.
  - 0: Settings (and/or not logged in page)
  - 1: Login page
  - 2: Logging in page
  - 3: Account Information page
